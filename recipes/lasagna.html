Lasagna

Description

Loaded with cheese and ricotta, Lasagna is an Italian cuisine loved by millons in the world.

If youre a lover of cheese and tomatoes, you would definitely enjoy the final result of this recipe.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Sugar
  • Seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Egg
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Start by making the meat sauce.
  2. Boil the lasagna noodles to cook.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil paper then bake.
  6. Serve the lasagna after it rests.
